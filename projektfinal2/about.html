About Us

KOHA BAKERY is a family-owned bakery founded in 2012. We specialize in handcrafted pastries and bread, baked fresh every morning with the finest ingredients. Every day, we prepare fresh products using traditional recipes and quality ingredients, so you can taste the comfort of home in every bite. From daily bread and savory pies to freshly made desserts, our commitment is to serve you with love and quality in everything we bake. Welcome to our bakery - where every day begins with the smell of something good!
